About me

My name is Corey Taylor and I am a musician, author and producer. I come from Iowa in the US. I currently work with my two bands Slipknot and Stone Sour. When I am not on tour with them I like to produce music and write books. As a producer I worked with bands like Walls Of Jericho or Facecage.
I consider myself a really straight person, I always speak my mind.
